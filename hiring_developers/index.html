The goal

Original process

Curriculum Vitae

First throw half of the CVs into the trash, we don't hire unlucky people.

Biases: Gender, age, race, name, photo, previous work places, schools, hobbies

Previous large employer > Not homogenous

Previous small employer > You don't know them

Techincal interview

Whiteboard coding: waste of time, not realistic

Lexical knowledge specific to your company: People will learn

Noisy process

We lost many good candidates

We hired people whose performance was clearly below standards even after 2 or 3 days.

How we improved

Curriculum Vitae

We only reject CVs with obvious red flags

Homework

Avoids wasting time with whiteboard coding

Instead of the CV we just provide years of experience and position.

Homework pitfals

Assessment days

Payed
