Lean Poker

A fun way to learn continuous deployment.

by Rafael Ordog (DeVill)

@devillsroom

Lean Poker is a full day workshop that focuses on fast and effective delivery.

Event outline

First hour

Short introduction

Teams are formed

Teams fork the player framework

The game sessions

A sit'n'go runs every 2 minutes for an hour

Before every sit'n'go the latest HEAD revisions are deployed

The winner gets 5 points, the runner up gets 3 points

The retro

Watch the last sit'n'go of the session

Discuss experiences

Short break while no one codes, and robots do not play

DEMO TIME

Things you can learn

The minimum viable product is less than you'd expect.

The importance of being first on the market

The advantages of a good delivery pipeline

The perks of a fast feedback loop

And a lot of other stuff...
